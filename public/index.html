Program Overview

The High School Option Program (HSOP) was designed to provide high schools students with the opportunity to start taking college courses at a 65 percent discount off of Rowan College's (RCGC) tuition and per credit fees.

Completed courses under the HSOP program can be applied to a RCGC associate degree program or easily transferred to other institutions of higher education.

Gain A Competitive Edge

  • Personalized advisement for course selection
  • Complete any required remediation
  • Satisfy general education electives
  • Chip away at degree requirements
  • Explore career pathways
  • Learn how to navigate higher education prior to HS graduation

Am I Eligible?

  • Students 15 years of age and older who have completed freshman year of high school
  • In county residents
  • Out of county residents *see out of county discounted rates
  • Students who attend:
    • Public school
    • Private school
    • Homeschool

Enrollment Is Easy

  1. Submit documents to RCGC's Office of Admissions:
    • High School Participation Form
    • High School Transcripts

    • Note: You may be exempt
